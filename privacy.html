Privacy Policy

Information We Collect

  • Information you choose to give us

  • Information we obtain when you use Buckets

Below you will find more information on these two types of data collection.

Information You Choose to Give Us

This includes only your Ohio State email address and phone number. The team at Buckets feels strongly about the privacy of our users. As such, we will never sell your personal information. The data given to us by our users is solely used to help those who use Buckets find the products they care about.

Information We Obtain When You Use Buckets

As you use Buckets, we will periodically collect information based on how you use our services. We use this information to improve Buckets and create a better experience for our users. This basic information is aggregated through all of our users and the information we obtain cannot be tracked back to the user that performed it. As mentioned before, we know our users care about their privacy, so we take special care in performing these actions.


How We Use This Information

We use the information we collect to better the experience of Buckets for our users. Whether it's adding/removing features, or changing the layout to make our apps easier to navigate, our sole goal is to create an easy-to-use marketplace for college students. For this reason, our Privacy Policy is very easy to understand: If we don't use information to better the experience for our users, we don't collect it in the first place.
